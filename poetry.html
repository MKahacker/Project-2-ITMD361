Writer's Block

I sat and couldn't think no more
My thoughts they walked straight out the door
And here I sat waiting for more
Staring at the paper till my eyes were soar
My notebook empty from A to Z
What could I do to rhyme with Z
I sat around and thought of me
My life and what I ought to be
Yet, the siege on my mind was unbroken
My mind was sleeping it seemed broken
I would stare at the paper and try to write
But my words seemed to be lost in flight
I waited waited and then a spark
I saw over there in the dark
Suddenly waves of ideas came to be
And my mind bloomed like the blossom tree
By Mahmoud Kahack

Sitting Side by Side

Sitting side by side
And I wouldn't have lied
If I said they were identical
Parts of the same tentical
Brick and mortar offsprings
Of the old rock minings
Sitting side by side
In the city and the countryside
